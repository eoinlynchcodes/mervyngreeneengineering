Mervyn Greene Engineering

Structural Steel. Fuel Tank Stands. Custom Solutions.

Mervyn Greene Engineering have been in business for almost 30 years. We work on all aspects of steel work - from the design stage, all the way through to erection of the finished product.

We do not allow convention limit what we can do. We gladly deliver on the most ambitious projects requiring novel, innovative solutions and new ways of doing things.

With supplier relationships built over many years, we use the best materials and fabrication processes. Our fabrication is done in house by MGE employees to ensure of the highest standard conforming to CE marking regulations 1090-1:2009+A1:2011. MGE Products, Multi-Safe Tank Stands, Structural Steel Manufacturing and Factory Processes are also CE certified.

MGE work with you to create custom solutions for whatever you need. Our customers range from private households to large construction firms.

Fuel Tank Stands.

Industrial. Domestic. Custom.

MGE developed Fuel Tank Stands for diesel tanks, domestic oil tanks and also build based on your requirements. All tank stands are galvanised and include fittings and necessary equiptment. Our tank stands are used in farm yards, construction sites, machinery yards and domestically. Slide out stairs ensure safety and ease of access.

We make stands to cater for one, two or three diesel tanks. Delivery or collection are available. Stands can be made for your specific tank and height requirements. Call us to discuss your best option.


This product was featured in the Irish Independent and the Irish Farmers Journal after winning awards for innovation and safety.
